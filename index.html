Repairs Info

All replacement parts are sourced from a reliable company and fitted with a 12 month warrenty

When replacing parts to your device, a great amount of car is put into making sure that you recieve the best possible repairs and leaving your device in a near new condition. This level of care is catered for in the costs of the repairs, and we will make sure that you are happy with the service you recieve. In most cases, there will be no evidence that your device has been opened, and will be returned to you in the same condition as you sent it to us in, however with the repairs completed.

Postage and Packing

Extra costs may apply

We take pride in making sure you are satisfied with the condition of your device when you recieve it. Postage to us will need to be covered by you, and on larger items - games consoles for example - will need to be covered by you. We make sure to use a suitable amount of packaging and a strong box when we return the item to you, in some cases your own packaging material will be re-used when sending it back to you, this will only happen when we are satisfied that your packaging will fully protect your device.
